Lesson roadmap

Beginner learning paths

01

Warm-up & posture

Start with neck rolls, shoulder circles, hip mobility, ankle warm-ups, and neutral posture. This prevents injury and improves line quality.

02

Rhythm & counting

Learn 4-count and 8-count timing. Clap the beat first, then add footwork, arms, expressions, and transitions.

03

Basic steps

Practice two foundation steps from your selected style every day. Keep movement clean before increasing speed.

04

Performance confidence

Use facial expression, eye focus, stage spacing, and musical pauses to make simple steps look professional.
