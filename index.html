Dance Styles

Explore dance forms from culture to stage.

Each style has its own rhythm, origin, attitude, and training method.

Bollywood

Expressive, energetic, colorful and perfect for beginners.

Hip-Hop

Groove, confidence, freestyle, rhythm and urban expression.

Classical

Discipline, posture, expressions, tradition and storytelling.

Contemporary

Fluid movement, emotion, balance and modern expression.

Salsa

Partner rhythm, timing, footwork and social dance confidence.

Ballet

Grace, flexibility, strength, alignment and stage elegance.

Folk Dance

Community, culture, celebration, tradition and regional identity.

Freestyle

Personal movement, creativity, confidence and self-expression.

Kathak

Footwork, spins, rhythm, expressions and Indian storytelling.

Bharatanatyam

Strong geometry, expressions, devotion and classical discipline.

Jazz

Sharp lines, musicality, stage performance and personality.

Zumba

Fitness, fun, stamina, rhythm and energetic movement.
